    About Stellar

    Stellar is a decentralized platform that makes use of a blockchain to make moving money fast and reliable. This hybrid blockchain is decentralized and completely open-source, so anyone can benefit from it. With the infrastructure behind Stellar, it is easy to transfer assets between users regardless of country and all users have the same economic participation and access.
